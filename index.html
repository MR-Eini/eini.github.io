Research Interests

Developing methods to ascertain the impacts climate change has on long-term surface and subsurface water storage, flood, drought, ecosystems, and crop yield using novel modeling techniques and the integration of advanced observation data. Performing modeling studies with the inclusion of local sustainable technologies using process-based and statistical hydrologic models.
